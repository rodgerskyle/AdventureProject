The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe goes with a threatening approach to make him let him out. With no luck, the supposed friend says no to him and Harambe is stuck in the zoo prison. When the guards come back, they notice the other gorilla was gone and they tighten up their security in the prison. Harambe loses hope because he never goes to see any opportunity to escape. He regretted never trying to reason with his friend. Harambe later dies from the amount of days locked up in the cell.
