The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe tells George how he was serious about him telling the girls how cool he was. George and Harambe become very good friends and do everything together from then on. One could only wonder though if the rude behavior towards George is the reason Harambe stays with George to hang out. George and Harambe live the rest of the life in the zoo with no other problems that occur. Now what would have happened if Harambe never became friends with George?... Harambe will never know because he dies of old age.
