The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe and George get in an all out brawl with each other over the banana being thrown. George runs up to Harambe and starts completely ravishing Harambe. George outsmarts Harambe in every hit by using more power when Harambe makes a mistake. At the end Harambe is completely bloodied up and really bruised up. Harambe gets up one last time and this is when George hits him so hard, Harambe falls back hitting his head on a trunk. Harambe doesn't get up after that and dies right where he stood.
