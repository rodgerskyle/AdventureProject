The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe runs away from whatever he had seen in the corner of his eye. Harambe manages to get to a peaceful spot in the woods. He is very alone until he notices a squirrel who speaks gorilla because he used to go to the same zoo Harambe was from. The squirrel and Harambe become very good friends and benefit each other. The squirrel could grab food for Harambe that he couldn't get to and Harambe was the squirrel's protector from other creatures. Harambe lives out his life in the Mount Ary Forest with his new found friend that was a squirrel.
