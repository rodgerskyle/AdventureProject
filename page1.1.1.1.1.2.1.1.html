The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe goes to check out the thing that was in the corner of his eye. He goes up to where it was and immediately a man comes out and tries to shoot Harambe. Harambe pushes the gun out of the man's hand and punches the man away. The man stumbles up and runs to his gun and manages to grab it and aim it up at Harambe. Harambe again manages to hit the gun but not before the man gets off a shot. Harambe knocks the man out and possibly kills him but Harambe slowly bleeds out and dies...
