The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe attempts to grab his friend that has helped him scheme the idea of escaping. Harambe grabs onto his friend and the friend throws Harambe into the guard. His friend, don't know if he can call him that anymore, but the friend escapes out of there. Harambe is locked up and from that moment on the guards don't go back into the cell. Harambe spends the rest of his life in there slowly rotting and soon after he dies. He never knew what happened to his friend but at least he did the right thing by trying to save his supposed friend.
