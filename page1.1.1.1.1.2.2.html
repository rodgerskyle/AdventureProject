The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe heads to the river that is close by the zoo in Cincinnati. Harambe makes his way to the river and looks over into the water. Harambe, with an urge to jump in the water and swim, goes straight into the water. Harambe forgot that he didn't know how to swim and starts to fall under. Harambe drowns after all that hard work to get out of the zoo prison, R.I.P. Harambe.
