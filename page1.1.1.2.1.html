The Adventures of Harambe

Harambe's new friend steals the guard's keys when the guard was giving him food and water. When the guard leaves, his friend unlocks the door for him and they both leave the prison. It is completely pitch black when they go outside but the other gorilla wishes Harambe luck in the world and he vanishes after that. Harambe looks around for anything that could be of use to him. He sees a lit up sign that looks to have a map on it, he goes over and figures out what he should do.
